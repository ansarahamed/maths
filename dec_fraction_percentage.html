🎓 Let's Learn: Fractions, Decimals & Percentages (with Pizza!)

🍕 Imagine a Pizza!

A pizza is a perfect way to learn math. Let’s say the pizza is cut into equal slices.

🟡 What is a Fraction?

A fraction shows a part of a whole.
It looks like this: 1/2, 1/4, 3/4

  • 1/2 means 1 out of 2 equal parts

  • 1/4 means 1 out of 4 equal parts

  • 3/4 means 3 out of 4 equal parts

🔢 Fractions to Decimals

Just divide the top number by the bottom number:

FractionDecimal
1/20.5
1/40.25
3/40.75

🎯 Decimals to Percentages

Multiply the decimal by 100:

DecimalPercentage
0.550%
0.2525%
0.7575%

🍕 Pizza Time Example

Fraction of PizzaDecimalPercentageWhat It Means

3/40.7575%You ate most of the pizza!

🧠 Mini Quiz Time! (Answer Below)

Q1: If you eat 1 slice out of 4, what fraction is that?
Q2: What is 3/4 as a percentage?
Q3: Which is bigger: 0.25 or 0.5?
Q4: You ate 75% of your chocolate bar. What fraction is that?

✅ Answers:

  1. 1/4

  2. 75%

  3. 0.5 is bigger

  4. 3/4
